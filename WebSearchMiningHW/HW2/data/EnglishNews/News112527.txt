Breakingviews - Walmart explores perks of being second best
NEW YORK (Reuters Breakingviews) - Walmart is late to the hot new trend of subscription-based shopping, but that isn’t such a bad thing. In September, the U.S. retailer launched a membership model akin to Amazon.com’s Prime. Like Walt Disney’s streaming service, which is quickly catching up to Netflix, Walmart may have the advantage of being able to ride on a more innovative rival’s coattails.
