Breakingviews - China’s news war risks collateral economic harm
HONG KONG (Reuters Breakingviews) - China’s war on U.S. newspapers could backfire economically. Beijing is expelling reporters from the Wall Street Journal, the New York Times and the Washington Post in retaliation for U.S. restrictions on Chinese state media. For foreign investors who are already inclined to discount local financial reporting, it’s another red flag. It also risks further inflating the fake news bubble enveloping President Xi Jinping.
