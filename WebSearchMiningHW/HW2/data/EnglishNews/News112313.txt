Breakingviews - Biden’s Cabinet picks can lean into free markets
SAN FRANCISCO (Reuters Breakingviews) - Joe Biden’s cabinet can benefit from leaning into the free market. Investors are flooding into companies that could bring both energy and transportation into a greener and more efficient future. That should help the U.S. president-elect’s nominees to lead those respective agencies, Jennifer Granholm and Pete Buttigieg, in their roles. That’s assuming they can lift – not implement – red tape.
