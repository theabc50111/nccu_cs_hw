Breakingviews - Google and Facebook are begging for a watchdog
WASHINGTON/NEW YORK (Reuters Breakingviews) - Google and Facebook have made their quarterly case for a watchdog. The search engine’s parent, Alphabet, reported soaring sales and profit just days after Facebook’s blowout earnings. Concerns about fake news and the wanton commercialization of consumer data have done little to clip their dominance of online advertising. It’s all the more reason for regulatory oversight.
