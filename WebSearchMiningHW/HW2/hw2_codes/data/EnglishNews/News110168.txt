Wells Fargo to sell asset management business for $2.1 bln
Feb 23 (Reuters) - Wells Fargo & Co said on Tuesday it had agreed to sell its asset management business, which manages more than $603 billion on behalf of customers, to private equity firms GTCR LLC and Reverence Capital Partners for $2.1 billion. (Reporting by Noor Zainab Hussain in Bengaluru; Editing by Aditya Soni)
