Breakingviews - Facebook has court of public opinion to woo, too
SAN FRANCISCO (Reuters Breakingviews) - Facebook faces two judgments in its U.S. antitrust battle – a legal one, and another in the court of public opinion. A multi-state lawsuit against the $807 billion social network launched on Wednesday could drag on for years. Meanwhile what matters most is ensuring that the company’s 3.2 billion users remain mostly unfazed.
