Blackstone-backed Patria could reach $1.6 billion valuation in IPO - BTG
SAO PAULO (Reuters) - Brazilian asset manager Patria Investments Ltd, which is backed by Blackstone Group Inc, is likely to reach a valuation of between $1 billion and $1.6 billion in its planned initial public offering on Nasdaq, Banco BTG Pactual SA said in a note to clients.
