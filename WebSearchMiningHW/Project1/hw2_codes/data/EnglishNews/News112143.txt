Breakingviews - Guest view: Dislocation will be 2021’s buzzword
PARIS (Reuters Breakingviews) - Dislocation will be the buzzword for 2021: of supply chains, credit and, above all, the labour market across the European, and global, economies. What we don’t know is how long-lasting and far-reaching the changes resulting from the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic’s economic aftermath will be. Many companies and workers are still in a state of limbo between the pre-virus world and the new (brave?) one. While the rollout of effective vaccines pushes us to look forward, the full extent of the shock is unknown.
