Breakingviews - Hostile buyer lets G4S escape its grasp
LONDON (Reuters Breakingviews) - GardaWorld’s pursuit of G4S has left it empty-handed. Five months after the Canadian group revealed its interest in the accident-prone UK security company, and at the start of a five-day auction overseen by London’s takeover regulator, Chief Executive Stephan Crétier has walked away. Rival Allied Universal’s 3.8 billion pound cash bid should allow it to capture a solid return.
