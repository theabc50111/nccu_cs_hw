Breakingviews - Chinese investors can soothe Xiaomi’s U.S. pains
HONG KONG (Reuters Breakingviews) - It’s good to be able to turn to friends for support. Chinese phone maker Xiaomi lost some $10 billion in market value following its addition to a U.S. blacklist of companies deemed to have military ties. Others already tagged, including China Mobile, have recently seen local buying ease the pain of U.S. selling. Xiaomi, a market darling, can expect something similar.
