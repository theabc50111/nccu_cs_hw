SoftBank-backed Coupang to raise as much as $3.6 bln in U.S. IPO 
March 1 (Reuters) - South Korean e-commerce giant Coupang Inc, which is backed by SoftBank Group Corp, will look to raise as much as $3.6 billion in its U.S. initial public offering, it said in a regulatory filing on Monday. (Reporting by Anirban Sen in Bengaluru; Editing by Sriraj Kalluvila)
