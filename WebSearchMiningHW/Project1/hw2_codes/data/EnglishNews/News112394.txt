Breakingviews - Green investing’s killer app faces growing pains
LONDON (Reuters Breakingviews) - Green investing may be on the brink of discovering its killer app. A growing number of climate finance mavens, including former Bank of England Governor Mark Carney, are increasingly excited by the potential of so-called “degree warming metrics”. Done properly, these could allow stakeholders to judge how well their investments align with the Paris accord. Yet with many different types available, a harmonised methodology ahead of next year’s Glasgow climate summit looks ambitious.
