Breakingviews - Intel woes provide timely lesson for Samsung
HONG KONG (Reuters Breakingviews) - Intel’s troubles today could be Samsung’s tomorrow. The South Korean giant is ending 2020 on a strong note, thanks largely to its dominance in memory chips. Its U.S. peer, by contrast, has fallen behind and faces calls from a pushy investor to break up. A prolonged leadership vacuum at Samsung threatens a similar gradual decline.
