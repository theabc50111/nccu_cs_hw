Breakingviews - Chancellor: Year of extraordinary popular delusion
LONDON (Reuters Breakingviews) - Last March this column argued that an epidemic of fear was travelling fast around the world on the coat-tails of Covid-19 and that the events of 2020 might well provide an additional chapter to Charles Mackay’s “Extraordinary Popular Delusions and the Madness of Crowds”. The intervening months have reinforced that view. The pandemic hasn’t just provoked irrational fears; a great speculative bubble has also appeared. Errors of optimism are now conjoined with errors of pessimism.
