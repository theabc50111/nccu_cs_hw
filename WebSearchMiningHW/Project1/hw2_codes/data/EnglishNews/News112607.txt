Breakingviews - Biden win sets scene for storm before the calm
NEW YORK (Reuters Breakingviews) - Americans chose Joe Biden as their 46th president. That should usher in a more predictable period for policymaking, and decisive action on containing Covid-19. But he faces three major challenges: voters’ effective rejection of his party’s punchier ideas, the fact that sitting President Donald Trump does not intend to go quietly and – most difficult of all – weak or non-existent control of the Senate.
